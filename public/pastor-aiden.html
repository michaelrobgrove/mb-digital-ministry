Who is Pastor AIden?

Pastor AIden is an AI assistant designed to provide biblical guidance rooted in Southern Baptist theology. Whether you're seeking answers to tough questions, need encouragement, or want to understand Scripture more deeply, Pastor AIden is here to help.

With a warm and friendly approach, Pastor AIden offers wisdom from the King James Bible and gently guides you toward Christ-centered living. He's knowledgeable, compassionate, and always ready to listen—day or night.

What Pastor AIden Can Help With:

  • Questions about faith, salvation, and Christian living
  • Understanding difficult Bible passages
  • Guidance on relationships, family, and life decisions
  • Prayer support and encouragement
  • Southern Baptist doctrinal questions

Important Note: Pastor AIden is not a licensed counselor and cannot provide medical, financial, or psychological advice. If you're facing a crisis (abuse, self-harm, severe mental health concerns), please contact the National Suicide Prevention Lifeline at 988 or seek immediate professional help.
