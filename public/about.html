Our Mission

The mission of Maryland Baptist Digital Ministry is to glorify God by reaching the world with the gospel of Jesus Christ. We are dedicated to providing accessible, biblically-sound resources that encourage spiritual growth, foster a Christ-centered community online, and equip believers to live out their faith with conviction and compassion.

Our Beliefs

The Scriptures: We believe the Holy Bible, consisting of the 66 books of the Old and New Testaments, is the inspired, inerrant, and infallible Word of God. It is the final authority for all matters of faith and practice.

God: We believe in one God, eternally existing in three persons: the Father, the Son (Jesus Christ), and the Holy Spirit. He is the Creator, Sustainer, and Ruler of all things.

Jesus Christ: We believe in the deity of our Lord Jesus Christ, in His virgin birth, in His sinless life, in His miracles, in His vicarious and atoning death through His shed blood, in His bodily resurrection, in His ascension to the right hand of the Father, and in His personal return in power and glory.

Salvation: We believe that salvation is by grace alone, through faith alone, in Christ alone. It is a free gift from God, not a result of any human works, and is received personally by repentance and faith in the Lord Jesus Christ.

The Church: We believe in the spiritual unity of believers in our Lord Jesus Christ, who are the body of Christ, the church. We are called to make disciples of all nations, baptizing them and teaching them to obey all that Christ has commanded.
