Our Mission

Maryland Baptist Digital Ministry exists to bring the timeless truths of God's Word into the digital age. We are committed to providing biblically sound teaching, pastoral care, and spiritual encouragement to believers wherever they are.

Through innovative use of technology and artificial intelligence, we aim to make quality biblical resources accessible 24/7, meeting people where they are in their spiritual journey.

What We Believe

Maryland Baptist Digital Ministry holds to the historic doctrines of the Christian faith as expressed in Southern Baptist theology:

  • Scripture: We believe the Bible, in its original manuscripts, is the inspired, inerrant, and authoritative Word of God. We use the King James Version as our primary translation.
  • The Trinity: We believe in one God eternally existing in three persons: Father, Son, and Holy Spirit.
  • Salvation: We believe salvation is by grace alone, through faith alone, in Christ alone. It is a free gift that cannot be earned by works.
  • The Church: We believe the church is the body of Christ, made up of all true believers. We affirm the autonomy of the local church.
  • Baptism and the Lord's Supper: We believe in believer's baptism by immersion and the Lord's Supper as symbolic ordinances established by Christ.
  • The Gospel: We believe Jesus Christ died for our sins, was buried, and rose again on the third day according to the Scriptures.

For a complete statement of faith, we affirm the Baptist Faith and Message 2000.

Meet Pastor AIden

Pastor AIden is an AI-powered pastoral assistant designed to provide biblical guidance, answer questions, and offer spiritual encouragement. While Pastor AIden uses advanced language models, all responses are carefully aligned with Southern Baptist theology and grounded in Scripture.

Important: Pastor AIden is not a replacement for a local church or a human pastor. We strongly encourage all believers to be actively involved in a Bible-believing local congregation.
